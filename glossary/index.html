AI Recruiting & Vetting Glossary

Key terms, definitions, and technical architectures behind automated candidate screening and semantic match routing.

What is this? This glossary explains the technical standards, compliance policies, and algorithmic concepts that power modern candidate matchmaking and telephone-based AI vetting systems.

Outbound Call Screening

Automated candidates qualification via outbound telephone calls using real-time voice synthesis and dynamic playbook logic.

Blended Semantic Score

Multi-weighted candidate evaluation based on resume context, best-matching sections, and geocoded local distance.

Fitment Gate

The multi-agent validation layer assessing candidate experience levels and checking knockout eligibility criteria.

Dynamic Playbook Generation

Automated synthesis of unstructured JDs into scoring rubrics, qualification criteria, and voice screen interview scripts.

Timezone-Aware Outbound

Regulatory safeguards that validate local candidate times via ZIP codes, placing calls strictly in TCPA calling windows.

Automated Scheduling

Post-call SMS and email integrations that directly link successful screens to Greenhouse, Google, and Outlook calendars.
