JobQual vs. Paradox.ai: Voice AI vs. SMS Chatbots

Comparing interactive phone screening and playbook grading with SMS chat threads. An honest look at candidate experience and vetting depth.

TL;DR. Paradox.ai is an enterprise chat-bot platform centered around "Olivia," which qualifies frontline and hourly candidates over SMS, WhatsApp, and web chat. It is highly optimized for fast, transactional chat intake. JobQual is a voice-first screening platform that automatically calls candidates to conduct interactive verbal phone screens. JobQual evaluates depth of experience using multi-agent rubrics, scores seniority, and handles outbound calls safely with geocoded local calling windows. If you need simple text-based chat gating for hourly retail/restaurant candidates, Paradox is a strong option. If you are screening for technical, professional, or corporate roles where verbal communication and depth of experience must be checked, JobQual is the right fit.
