JobQual vs. HireVue: AI Phone Screens vs. Video Vetting

Comparing telephone voice screening with asynchronous one-way video assessments. An honest analysis of candidate drop-off and evaluation methods.

TL;DR. HireVue is an industry pioneer in enterprise asynchronous video interviews. Candidates record video responses to prompts, which are scored by HireVue's AI models against job criteria. It is built for massive enterprise intake. JobQual is a telephone-first screening platform. It initiates automated phone screens, allowing candidates to engage verbally in a natural conversation. JobQual geocodes calling windows, validates seniority rubrics, and books calendars. If you require video-recorded assessments, proctored coding challenges, and have a massive budget (Essentials start at $35,000/year), HireVue is the enterprise standard. If you want to screen candidates over the phone with 80% lower candidate drop-off and integrate directly with Greenhouse, JobQual is the right fit.

Comparison at a Glance
