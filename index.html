From resume to booked interview in three steps

JobQual handles the full top-of-funnel — no recruiter involvement until the candidate is pre-qualified and scheduled.

1

Resumes in, playbooks out

Upload job descriptions (PDF, DOCX, plain text). JobQual ingests the JD, extracts location and requirements, and assembles a custom screening playbook: knockout questions, rubric weights, seniority framing, and semantic job embeddings for candidate matching.

2

AI calls. Candidates answer.

The outbound dialing engine checks TCPA calling windows, verifies numbers against DNC lists, then places the call. The AI agent runs the playbook — knockout gates first, then role-specific questions — and scores every answer in real time against your rubric.

3

Scored, scheduled, synced

Qualified candidates receive a calendar booking link by SMS and email. ICS invites are auto-generated. Scorecards, transcripts, and recordings push to your ATS (Greenhouse or webhook). Your team reviews a ranked shortlist — not a pile of unvetted resumes.

Custom screening playbooks

Each job gets a tailored playbook: knockout questions (auto-gated), scored technical probes, a seniority framing layer, and a rubric calibrated to the role. Upload a JD and JobQual drafts the first version automatically.

Scorecard & knockout gating

Every call produces a structured scorecard: per-question rubric scores, an aggregate fitment score, knockout verdicts, and a recruiter-readable summary. Candidates who fail a knockout are gated out automatically.

Semantic resume matching

JobQual embeds resumes and job descriptions and scores candidate-to-role fit using a blended model: whole-resume semantic similarity, a section-best score (to surface hidden-gem candidates), and a Haversine location bonus. Reverse matching shows each candidate's best-fit roles.

Common questions

How does the outbound screening flow work?

The dialing engine checks the candidate's local time (resolved from their ZIP code) against TCPA calling windows (8 AM–9 PM), verifies the number against the global and tenant DNC lists, then places the call. The AI agent introduces itself with the recruiter's disclosure script, runs the playbook — knockouts first — and scores each answer in real time. When the call ends, a scorecard, summary, and transcript are generated and pushed to the recruiter portal and ATS.

Is JobQual TCPA compliant?

Yes. TCPA compliance is enforced at the infrastructure level — not just policy. Calling-window validation runs on every outbound attempt. DNC checks happen before every dial. Consent records (including disclosure version and jurisdiction) are stored per candidate. Calls outside the allowed window are queued and retried at the next valid time.

How does the resume-to-job matching work?

JobQual embeds both resumes and job descriptions using a 1536-dimensional semantic model. The blended match score weights whole-resume similarity (70%), a section-best score that looks at individual resume sections against the JD (25%), and a location proximity bonus using Haversine great-circle distance from geocoded coordinates (5%). A reranker pass refines the top results. Reverse matching also works: given a candidate, JobQual returns their best-fit open roles.

What ATS integrations are available?

JobQual has a native Greenhouse connector (using the Harvest and Ingestion APIs) and a generic HMAC-SHA256 webhook connector that works with any ATS or HRIS that accepts webhooks. Scorecards, transcripts, audio recordings, and structured rubric payloads push automatically on screen completion. CSV export is also available for offline reporting.

How do staffing agencies submit candidates?

Recruiters generate a vendor magic link per job description — or a tenant-level link for general submissions. Agencies receive the URL and upload resumes directly (PDF, DOCX, or TXT — up to 20 files, 10 MB each). No login or portal access is required. JobQual parses contacts via LLM extraction, embeds the resume, and scores it against the target job automatically.

Does JobQual handle scheduling after the screen?

Yes. When a candidate passes the playbook, JobQual sends a calendar booking link by SMS and email. ICS invites are generated to RFC 5545 spec. The reminder dispatcher sends automated reminders at 24 hours and 1 hour before the interview slot, via both email and SMS, with idempotency guards to prevent duplicates.
