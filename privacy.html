Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2026. Your privacy and candidate data compliance details.

1. Data Collection and Usage

JobQual Technologies, Inc. collects resume data, applicant profiles, and audio recordings/transcripts of outbound voice screens solely for candidate qualification as authorized by our customer organizations.

2. Data Protection

All candidate submissions, resume vectors, and voice records are encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell or trade personal information to third-party data brokers.

3. Regulatory Compliance

We run outbound phone calls strictly in compliance with TCPA and local calling hour boundaries. Candidate timezone calculations are geocoded using ZIP codes, and telephone numbers are reconciled against active DNC block-lists.
